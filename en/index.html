SILVER PaLM

Silver PaLM is a technical company that provides services in the implementation of innovative technological solutions and in the elaboration of technical projects in the whole range of industrial and productive enterprises.

Our principle is to provide its customers with high-quality solutions and equipment at an affordable cost in order to maximize the profitability of their businesses.

SILVER PaLM manages turnkey solutions as we can provide consulting services, develop technical and economic studies, process licensing, provide innovative and specialized materials and equipment and implement projects.

SILVER PaLM is  staffed by engineers as well as economists with long experience and high level of expertise in the fields of its activity. Know-how, strong customer-centric perception, flexibility and efficiency provide direct, innovative and cost-effective solutions that respond to the significance of time and the specialisation of modern business environment.

SERVICES

Consulting Services

We provide our clients with consulting services for a wide range of topics such as:

  • Development of a feasibility study
  • Implement start-ups for new businesses
  • Carrying out loan procedures
  • Integration into development laws and funding programs
Design of technical studies

We undertake technical and environmental studies

Upgrade / improve production process

We study and implement the upgrading and optimization of production processes required due to changes in legislation, to the existence of chronic quality problems or just to improve the economy and functionality. The services include need analysis, solution design under Capacity Management and suggestions for purchasing and/or upgrading of necessary equipment.

Permits integration

We undertake the preparation of the required technical and environmental studies, the preparation of the files and the processing of the Permits of any industrial or business activity. The extensive experience of our executives and their technical training guarantees fast and efficient procedures.

Execution of technical works

We undertake the development of technical projects focusing on the installation of Electro / Mechanical systems, projects in the oil and energy sector, fire-fighting systems, energy saving projects, application of innovative building materials.

Project Management

We undertake the management of projects related to our expertise on behalf of our clients. Management includes conducting studies, managing subcontractors, managing quality control and deliverables and, in general,implementing effectively projects within the cheduled time, quality and cost.

Supplies

We make every necessary supply of equipment, materials and spare parts on behalf our customers.

Education

We provide training on operation, handling and safety of electromechanical systems, as well as theoretical and practical training of their users, guaranteeing the economic and seamless operation of the company.
