Industrial & Warehousing

Industrial facilities and massive warehousing projects will find the solution in the Arch Steel Buildings range of models. Operating in the market of construction we have delivered facilities for various industries. Oil refineries, production sites, car repair units, food process units are some of the projects we have accomplished. Buildings are widely used in the grain, wheat and salt storage facilities among hundreds of other industries.

Bigger space is needed for a industrial unit to be productive and efficient. Safe storage, is important and Arch steel technology is one of the most best solutions in the market offering, endurance, dry storage, no beam spaces, high roofing and expansion opportunity.
