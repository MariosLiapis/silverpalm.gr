Green Ocean

GREEN OCEAN provides a full range of 100% eco-friendly products for spill control.

GREEN OCEAN specializes in solutions for many environmental challenges introducing a revolutionary mineral absorbent made of natural non-asbestos mineral fibers with nano-reinforcement. Unique Green Ocean filler is used for prevention and dealing with many environmental challenges:

  • oil spills,
  • wastewater processing,
  • oil sludge treatment,
  • industrial cleaning prevention,
  • water evaporation prevention.

 

Green Ocean’s unique patented filler ensures:

  • highest absorption in the market 1:50
  • best result in any temperature
  • long lasting period of use
  • proven oleophilic material

 

GREEN OCEAN believes that many damages can be prevented with easy and cost-effective natural solutions.

3 TIMES MORE OIL

GO delivers extraordinary value by absorbing three times more oil than polypropylene with 100% saturation. 120g pillow absorbs up to 4kg of spilled oil.

 

3 TIMES FASTER

99% of the absorption takes place in the first 15 minutes

 

95% OF OIL CAN BE RECOVERED

Cut the cost of wasted oil. You can recover 95 % of the oil by squeezing the material under pressure.

 

Green Ocean Products are certified by CEDRE (Research and Experimentation on Accidental Water Pollution). Production is ISO 9001/ISO 14001 certified. For further information visit http://greenocean.nl/
